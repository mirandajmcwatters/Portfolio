Creating a Family Farm Database

The database structure created in MySQL includes 16 tables. These tables allow data to be recorded for all major aspects of the farm: personnel, operations, and sales. Personnel and their associated information are captured in the Employee table. The operations features include tables to track land, crops, and harvests as well as the expenses associated with the farm. Sales tables allow for tracking of customer and sales data and income from the crops, including details of the channels used, which can provide key insights on where the farm can maximize revenues.
